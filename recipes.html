Pasta Salad Recipe

This pasta salad recipe is easy to make. It takes under 20 minutes from start to finish.

You will need: One pound dried pasta like fusilli, penne, rotini or farfalle, 1 cup halved cherry tomatoes, 1 cup thinkly sliced zucchini,cup (6 ounces) fresh mozzarella balls, chopped and 1/3 cup thinly sliced scallions (5 to 6).

DIRECTIONS
  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook until tender, 6 to 10 minutes.
  2. Drain and rinse well under cold water.
  3. Stir in the bell pepper, zucchini, tomatoes, scallions, pepperoncini, mozzarella, and the herbs.
  4. Taste for seasoning and adjust with salt and pepper as needed. Serve or for the best results, cover and refrigerate at least 30 minutes and up to 5 days.
